HOBBIES

As someone who changes hobbies every three to five business days, here are some of my favorite ways to kill time :D

DRAWING

Drawing is something I have always love to do ever since I was a kid. Some people suggest that I should pursue an art-related major, but I think I enjoy drawing more when I do it purely for entertainment

ORIGAMI

"Origami" refers to the art of folding paper, orginally from Japan. This is not something I am passionate about, so I only choose to make things that take less than 10 minutes to learn lol. Even though I only do basics stuff, I find it very relaxing and therapeutic

PLAY-DOH

Somehow it takes me hours to make the most simple stuff. Though it is fun, I only touch it like one every six months :/
